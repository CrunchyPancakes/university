What is INFO?

About the School of Information Management

The School of Information Management at Victoria University of Wellington offers a Bachelor of Commerce degree with an Information Systems major, BCom (INFO). Information Systems (IS) is the academic discipline that investigates how people and organisations use information and how IS and technology affect people, organisations and society. Information is a crucial resource and how we manage it affects every part of of life so come learn how to use information technology (IT) to solve business, organisation and societal problems and get the skills to manage information, technology and people in complex environments

What is a degree?

Undergraduate degrees are also known as Bachelor’s degrees and take three or four years of full-time study to complete.

What does a typical degree require?

  • Major in at least one subject (this is the main subject you’ll focus on; e.g. Information Systems), and
  • Complete 360 points worth of courses (around 120 points each year).

What is a major?

Majors are the main subject(s) you’ll focus on throughout your degree. For example, you can take a Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) with a major in Information System.
